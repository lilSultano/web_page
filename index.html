THE PLATFORM

Every city has blind spots.
IGUDAR finds them.

A piazza protected by three bollards on the main road. An attacker who takes a side street no one considered.

Cities defend the obvious routes. IGUDAR maps every possible approach, including the ones that seem irrelevant, and identifies the corridors an intelligent attacker would actually exploit.

A security consultant who places barriers by experience. But experience doesn't account for 200 possible approach angles.

IGUDAR simulates thousands of attack scenarios using real vehicle physics. It finds the gaps that human intuition misses and recommends placements that are mathematically optimal.

A city that spends €200,000 on barriers. But has never tested them against a determined attacker.

IGUDAR's adversarial AI attacks the city's own defenses with an intelligent Red Agent. If the defense fails in simulation, it fails before anyone gets hurt. Not after.

02

HOW IT WORKS

From raw city data
to validated defense.

01

Ingest

Upload your city. IGUDAR loads real-world geographic data and builds a precise digital twin of the urban environment at sub-meter resolution.

02

Analyze

The platform identifies every corridor an attacker could use to reach the target. Not just the obvious ones. Every street, every passage, every vulnerability.

03

Defend

An AI agent places barriers in the optimal configuration. Each placement adapts to the changing tactical landscape. The defense evolves as corridors close.

04

Validate

A simulated attacker tries to breach the defense. If it fails, the defense works. You get a tactical report with casualty projections, velocity analysis, and regulatory compliance.

OUR VISION

Defend anywhere.

IGUDAR takes its name from the igudar — the fortified granaries of the Amazigh people in southern Morocco. Built into mountainsides above Taroudant, they protected entire communities for centuries. Not with armies, but with architecture.

Our mission is the same: use the geometry of the environment itself as a defense. An adversarial optimization engine that adapts to any scenario — from a market square to a commercial port, from a stadium to a government building.
